Figure No 1. Official Seal Source: Barangay Pamanlinan

The official seal of the Barangay Pamanlinan features important symbols that represent the livelihood, natural resources, and identify of the community. at the center of the seal is tree along with a account tree, symbolizing the barangay's connection to agriculture and its abundant natural environment. A shrimp is also prominently displayed, represents fishing and aquaculture as one of the main sources of income, especially in parts of the barangay such as Purok 5 & 6 where fishponds are located. The green in the seal signifies growth, fertility, and the community's strong ties to nature, while the color yellow represents hope, optimism, and a bright future for the people of Pamanlinan. Overall, the seal's design reflects the simplicity of rural life, sustainable livelihoods, and close relationship of the people with their natural surroundings.
